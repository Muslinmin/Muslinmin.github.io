Project Objective

Develop an automated food printing system using the EvEBot food print pen, to meet the branding needs of artisanal patisseries.

Project Introduction

Stakeholder Engagement: Conducted stakeholder interviews with Nicher, an artisanal patisserie, to understand their needs.

Needs:

  • Brand Awareness: Identified the benefits of printing edible brand images on pastries to enhance brand visibility for the business, its clients, and walk-in customers.
  • Need for Automation: Recognized the necessity for automation, given the nature of the EvEBot print pen and the existing workflow and manpower of the patisserie.

Our Solution:

Brand Image Printed by our solution

Our solution functionalities

Camera Guided Printer

  • Does not need pre-arrangement of pastries on tray to print
  • Able to print on pastries of beige colour and multi-colour (that is not too contrasting e.g. beige and brown)

Autonomous

  • The solution prints the whole tray for the baker
  • Fit into the workflow where the baker can load the trays. The clerk collects the trays on the other end.
